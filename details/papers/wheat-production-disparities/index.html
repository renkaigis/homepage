Identifying climate and environmental determinants of spatial disparities in wheat production using a geospatial machine learning model

AuthorsKai Ren, Yongze Song, L. Li, F. Mancini, Z. Xiao, X. Zhang, R. Qu, Qiang Yu

JournalGIScience & Remote Sensing, 2025

https://doi.org/10.1080/15481603.2025.2533487

Abstract

This paper uses a geospatial machine learning model to identify climate and environmental determinants of spatial disparities in wheat production, with evidence from Australian agricultural systems.
