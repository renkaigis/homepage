A spatio-temporal unmixing with heterogeneity model for the identification of remotely sensed MODIS aerosols: Exemplified by the case of Africa

AuthorsL. Yang, P. Luo, Z. Zhang, Yongze Song, Kai Ren, C. Zhang, J. Awange, P. M. Atkinson, L. Meng

JournalInternational Journal of Applied Earth Observation and Geoinformation, 2024

https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jag.2024.104068

Abstract

This co-authored paper proposes a spatio-temporal unmixing model with heterogeneity for identifying remotely sensed MODIS aerosols, exemplified by the case of Africa.
