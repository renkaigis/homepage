Lake Water Quality Visualization

Visualization of Chl-a Concentration in Dianchi Lake

This module visualizes Chl-a concentration in Dianchi Lake by combining discrete lake grids, initial concentration data, and hydrodynamic conditions such as wind direction, wind speed, inflow, and outflow.

Project Focus

Grid-based simulation and visualization of chlorophyll-a concentration dynamics in Dianchi Lake.

Methods

Discrete point grids, hydrodynamic condition coupling, concentration calculation, and VTK-based visualization.
