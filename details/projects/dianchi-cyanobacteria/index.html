Lake Hydrodynamic Modeling

Remote Sensing Monitoring and Early Warning System of Cyanobacteria Bloom in Dianchi Lake

This project simulates the spatial distribution and dynamic evolution of cyanobacteria bloom drift in Dianchi Lake and supports remote sensing monitoring and early warning.

Data

Remote sensing retrieval outputs, lake hydrodynamic data, FVCOM model outputs, and grid-based bloom layers.

Methods

Fortran, C#, VTK, ArcGIS Engine, FVCOM model coupling, particle drift simulation, and layered grid visualization.

Impact

Supports monitoring, warning, and visualization of cyanobacteria bloom drift across Dianchi Lake.

Status

Completed as a principal project for Kunming Environmental Monitoring Center.
