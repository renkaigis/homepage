Biography

Academic Background

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Nanjing Normal University. My work focuses on geospatial analysis, climate change and agriculture, spatial interpolation prediction of meteorological elements, GIS modeling and simulation, lake hydrodynamic numerical model simulation, and front-end/back-end GIS software systems.

I was a Research Associate at Curtin University from 2023.03 to 2025.03. I welcome collaborations on agricultural GIS, spatial prediction, climate impacts on production systems, and applied geospatial modeling.
