Research Agenda

Research

My research asks how geospatial data, spatial prediction, and GIS modeling can improve climate-agriculture analysis, environmental monitoring, and applied decision support.

Geospatial Analysis and Spatial Prediction

Spatial interpolation prediction of meteorological elements, spatial heterogeneity, outlier patterns, and geospatial machine learning for prediction and explanation.

Climate Change and Agriculture

Climate and environmental determinants of agricultural production, wheat production disparities, and GIS-supported agricultural decision systems.

GIS Modeling and Simulation

GIS modeling and simulation, lake hydrodynamic numerical modeling, remote sensing monitoring, and front-end/back-end GIS software system development.

Methods

Research Toolkit

Data

Meteorological observations, crop and field survey data, remote sensing imagery, hydrodynamic data, and GIS layers.

Models

Spatial interpolation, geospatial machine learning, spatial outlier analysis, GIS modeling, FVCOM-based simulation, and visualization.

Outputs

Peer-reviewed papers, agricultural decision systems, GIS software, simulation tools, conference reports, and reproducible analysis workflows.
